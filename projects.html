Academic Projects/Accomplishments

This is a solar oven I designed and built for my physics class. We were challenged to research a specific country and, using materials that are found in that country, construct a solar oven to help the poeple of that country get by easier. The oven reached a maximum temperature of 100 degrees fahrenheit which is much more than anticipated and completion took several days of planning on cad software, building itself, and numerous trials of testing.


Another project I did was build a windmill. This again was for physics and we used the same tactic of researching a country that goes along with the windmill to help that country out with the windmill. For the windmill, we resaerched blade designs and basically everything about a windmill including gear ratio and how the generator really works. Multiple trials were done to test the best material shape and number of blades for the windmill.
